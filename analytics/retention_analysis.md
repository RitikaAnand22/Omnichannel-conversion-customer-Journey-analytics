# Funnel Analysis – Order Conversion by Marketing Channel

## Objective
To identify where users drop off in the purchase journey and determine which marketing channels drive the highest order conversion.

## Funnel Steps
1. Online Orders (Entry Point)
2. Product Views vs Cart Additions (Comparison Step)
3. Total Orders (Conversion)

## Methodology
- Funnel built in Adobe Customer Journey Analytics
- Counted as: Unique Users
- Segmentation: Marketing Channel
- Time Range: Last 30 days

## Key Insights
- Cart Addition is the largest drop-off point in the funnel
- Path 2 (Product Views → Cart Additions → Orders) shows the highest conversion rate (63.2%)

## Business Interpretation
The funnel highlights friction at the cart stage, indicating opportunities to improve checkout experience.
